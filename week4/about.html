Our Story

Savory Spoon began not in a kitchen, but in a heart full of passion. In the bustling city of Lahore in 2010, a young culinary enthusiast, Adeel Khan, dreamed of a place where tradition and innovation could meet on a single plate.

What started as a family-run endeavor soon became a haven for food connoisseurs seeking soulful flavors and heartfelt hospitality. With each dish came stories — from secret spices passed down generations to fusion twists born from travel and curiosity.

We welcome you not just to dine, but to be part of our story — one rich with memories, devotion, and a promise to always serve food made with integrity and heart.

Meet Our Chefs

Chef Adeel Khan

Founder and Executive Chef, Adeel combines traditional Pakistani cuisine with French techniques. His philosophy? “Cook like you’re writing a poem.” Adeel turned a family recipe notebook into a culinary movement.

Chef Fatima Zahra

Born and raised in Casablanca, Chef Fatima brings the vibrant soul of North African spices. Known for her dramatic saffron risotto and harissa-glazed lamb, she sees food as storytelling on a plate.

Chef Abid Ali

Chef Bilal is a culinary poet from Istanbul whose passion lies in Ottoman-era flavors. His dishes echo Istanbul’s bazaars — bold, aromatic, and soul-warming. A storyteller at heart, he blends faith, history, and flavor.

Our Mission

At Savory Spoon, we are more than a restaurant — we are curators of culture, memories, and meaning. We serve not just food, but the warmth of family, the colors of tradition, and the aroma of innovation.

We are committed to excellence, sustainability, and inclusivity. Our kitchen is our canvas, and every meal we serve is crafted with reverence, soul, and a sprinkle of love.
